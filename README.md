# Sol LeWitt Structure Simulator

I was fascinated by the artwork and vibecoded a simulator of it with Claude Code.

## Controls

- **Orbit mode** — Click and drag to rotate, scroll to zoom
- **FPS mode** — Enable in settings, then click to lock. WASD to move, mouse to look, ESC to exit
- **P** — Take a screenshot
- **Settings** (gear icon) — Adjust cube size, camera height, light position, shadows, and toggle FPS mode
- **Mobile** — Touch drag to look around in FPS mode, on-screen WASD buttons to move
